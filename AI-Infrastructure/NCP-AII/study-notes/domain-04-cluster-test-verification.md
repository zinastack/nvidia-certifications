# Domain 04: Cluster Test And Verification

## Weight

33% of the exam blueprint.

## Summary

This domain validates whether the cluster is ready for production AI workloads
by testing compute, fabric, cabling, firmware, burn-in stability, and storage.

## Key Topics

- Single-node stress testing.
- HPL execution.
- Single-node NCCL and NVLink Switch verification.
- Cable signal quality and cabling correctness.
- Firmware and software checks on switches, BlueField-3, and transceivers.
- ClusterKit multifaceted node assessment.
- NCCL bandwidth validation across the east-west fabric.
- NCCL, HPL, and NeMo burn-in.
- Storage testing.

## Review Questions

1. What does HPL prove that NCCL does not?
2. What does NCCL reveal about the east-west fabric?
3. Why should firmware and cabling be verified before burn-in?
