# Domain 05: Troubleshoot And Optimize

## Weight

12% of the exam blueprint.

## Summary

This domain focuses on identifying faults, replacing failed components, and
optimizing servers and storage.

## Key Topics

- Identify and troubleshoot GPU, fan, and network card faults.
- Identify faulty cards, GPUs, and power supplies.
- Replace faulty cards, GPUs, and power supplies.
- Execute performance optimization for AMD and Intel servers.
- Optimize storage.

## Review Questions

1. What signals distinguish a GPU fault from a driver issue?
2. What evidence should be collected before replacing a component?
3. Which storage metrics help guide optimization?
